Frequently Asked Questions

Are these truly 8K resolution?

Yes. While AI models natively generate 1K-2K images, every image in these packs goes through a rigorous AI upscaling process to achieve a true, print-ready 7680x4320 (8K) resolution without losing intricate details.

Do I need specific software to use these?

No. The assets are delivered as standard high-resolution .JPG or .PNG files in a compressed .ZIP folder. They can be imported into any editing software like Premiere Pro, CapCut, Photoshop, or Canva immediately.

Are these safe for commercial use on YouTube?

Absolutely. Once you purchase the pack, you have full rights to use these backgrounds in your monetized YouTube videos, Instagram Reels, or physical print projects without any copyright claims.
